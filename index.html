A comparative digital edition

“To Autumn” was composed between September 19 and 21, 1819 and is today considered one of the great poems in English literature. The poem as it is read today has undergone a series of transformations and corrections since it was first put to paper. This digital edition contains the standard version of the poem alongside two earlier versions written in Keats’s hand. It is the goal of this edition to enable casual and scholarly readers of the poem to see the creative process at work between these versions of the poem, demonstrating how Keats crafted what was to become one of the most quoted poems in the English language.
